Introduction

In this lesson, you'll learn how to round to a specific number of decimal places. This is a skill used all the time in real life situations. For example:

  • Money and budgeting: Money always needs to be rounded to 2 decimal places
  • Science and medicine: Rounding measurements or dosages to a specific number of decimal places for accuracy and safety.
  • Construction and engineering: Rounding measurements (like lengths or volumes) for practical use in plans and material orders.

We will work entirely at your own pace. We'll start with some examples to demonstrate the process, then you'll have an opportunity to practice yourself. If you're finding things too difficiult at first, don't worry! I'll provide you with feedback to help you improve, and I won't move on until you're ready.
